                      About This Blogsite



What is Blog?

A blog (a shortened version of “weblog” is an online journal or informational website displaying information in the reverse chronological order, with the latest posts appearing first, at the top. It is a platform where a writer or a group of writers share their views on an individual subject.

Why Blog?

Blogging is a fun activity – many of us know that by now. And you, of all people, should know that, considering that you’re currently reading this article on the internet. With blogging, gone are the days that only prominent and professional people can speak their mind for the public to see. There’s just something about sharing your life and your experiences with other people that feels both fulfilling and satisfying. For some, it’s more than just a hobby. It’s a lucrative business all by itself.
